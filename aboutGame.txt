List of files:  main.cpp

				CommonFunction.c, CommonFunction.h
				ExplosionObject.c, ExplosionObject.c

				BaseObject.c, BaseObject.h
				ThreatsObject.c, ThreatsObject.h
				
You are on an plane and your mission is to kill enemy as many as possible and dont let be died. Game is over when you
hit on enemy or be taken down by their ammo. You cannot fly too low or pass over the border of screen. 
Use arrows(UP, DOWN, LEFT, RIGHT) to move, lef_click for laser and right-click for sphere.
